title: Main Headline

The shift away from fossil fuels and towards renewables is well underway, so why aren’t Scotland’s people feeling the benefit?

Scotland has a rich industrial heritage and some of the best natural resources in the world, but the wealth created by the shift away from fossil fuels is being quietly siphoned away from the communities closest to renewable infrastructure. 

Our Power calls on the Scottish government to build an energy system that prioritises workers, the public and the planet over corporations and investors. We were tasked with creating a campaign brand that spoke to unions, climate groups and communities, making the case for community ownership and a fair deal for oil & gas workers.
